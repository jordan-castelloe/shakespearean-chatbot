Othello Chatbot

Introduction

This is an interactive adaptation of Shakespeare's Othello. You play Iago, the central character and villain of the story. In the original text, Iago ruins people's lives for fun by withholding information and lying.

Instructions

  • You'll get messages from other characters. These are based on lines of dialogue from the play.
  • You can respond to each message by either telling the truth (the yellow box) or telling a lie (the blue box).
  • The truth isn't always the kindest option.
  • Your choice affects the characters' responses and the overall story arc.
  • There's no winning or losing, just different endings.

This is under construction

Right now the first act is up, but it's still kind of buggy. Here are some things to watch out for:

  • Please don't send your response until the character has finished typing.
  • If you type text into the yellow truth box, the program will think you're telling the truth, no matter what you type.
  • Same goes for the lie box.
  • If you want the story to make sense, your response should have similar meaning to the suggestion in the box. You can use whatever phrasing you want.

Cast of Characters

Iago (You)

You're the villain of the play. Cold and calculating, you serve under Othello and pretend to love him while secretly plotting against him.

Roderigo

One of Desdemona's jilted suitors and your co-conspirator in ruining Othello and Desdemona's marriage.

Othello

Your commanding officer and a good man. Othello is deeply in love with his new wife, Desdemona.

Desdemona

A bright and trusting young woman. Her father is Brabantio, a Venetian senator. She married Othello in secret just before the story starts.

Michael Cassio

Cassio is a young and handsome lieutenant under Othello's command.

Emilia

Your wife, who you've treated badly. She's smart, cynical, and distrustful of men. She becomes one of Desdemona's attendants.
